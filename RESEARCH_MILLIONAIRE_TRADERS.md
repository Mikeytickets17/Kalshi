## KELLY CRITERION POSITION SIZING

Formula: f* = (bp - q) / b

Where:
- f* = fraction of bankroll to bet
- b = net odds (payout ratio minus 1)
- p = probability of winning
- q = probability of losing (1 - p)

Example: Buy YES at 40c, probability of winning = 65%
- b = (1.00 / 0.40) - 1 = 1.5
- p = 0.65, q = 0.35
- f* = (1.5 × 0.65 - 0.35) / 1.5 = 0.417 (41.7% of bankroll)

But we use HALF KELLY (20.8%) to reduce variance.
With $1,000 bankroll: bet $208 per trade at this edge.
